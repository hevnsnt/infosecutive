SixCyber Security Policy

At SixCyber, we take your privacy and security seriously. If you discover a vulnerability or security issue affecting our services, we encourage you to report it to us directly using the contact information provided in our security.txt.

Responsible Disclosure

We appreciate the responsible disclosure of security issues. Please avoid testing vulnerabilities in a way that could disrupt our services or compromise user data.

Confidentiality of Your Report

Any information shared with us in the course of reporting a vulnerability will be treated as confidential. We will never sell, share, or disclose your contact information or any details of your report to third parties.

Your report and any personal details you provide are used solely for internal investigation and remediation by SixCyber staff. We will not use this information for any purpose beyond addressing the security issue you reported.

Scope

This policy applies to all websites and services operated by SixCyber unless otherwise stated. If you're unsure whether an issue is in scope, please reach out before testing.

Thank You

We are grateful to the security community for helping us improve the safety of our services.
