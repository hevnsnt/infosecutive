Security & Privacy Policy

At SixCyber, we are committed to maintaining a secure and trustworthy online environment. This policy outlines our approach to responsible vulnerability disclosure and how we handle personal data collected through our website.

Security Disclosure Policy

Reporting Vulnerabilities

We welcome responsible disclosure of vulnerabilities. Please report any suspected issues to security@sixcyber.com. Our security.txt file provides our full disclosure policy and contact details.

Confidentiality

Any information shared with us as part of a security report will be treated as confidential. Your personal data will not be shared or published without your explicit consent.

Responsible Testing

Please avoid testing methods that could impact service availability or expose user data. We appreciate the opportunity to investigate and remediate issues prior to public disclosure.

Privacy Policy

What We Collect

We collect limited, non-identifying information through analytics tools to better understand how visitors interact with our site. This includes IP addresses, browser types, device identifiers, and page visits.

Third-Party Analytics

We use Google Analytics and Cloudflare Analytics to collect aggregated usage data. These tools help us improve performance, security, and usability, but they may set cookies or collect anonymized technical data.

  • Google Analytics: Data may be transferred to and stored on Google servers. See Google's Privacy Policy.
  • Cloudflare Analytics: Data is used solely for operational insights and is not shared or sold by Cloudflare. See Cloudflare's Privacy Policy.

Your Data

We do not sell, rent, or share personal information submitted to us (such as via contact forms or email) with third parties. All data collected is used only for internal operations and communications related to your inquiry or interaction.

Your Options

You can disable cookies through your browser settings to limit data collection by analytics tools. If you contact us directly, you may request that we delete any personal data shared with us.

Security of Your Information

We take appropriate measures to protect all collected data using industry-standard practices including access control, encryption, and secure hosting infrastructure.

Policy Changes

This policy may be updated periodically. Changes will be posted on this page with a revised effective date.

Contact

For questions about this policy or to report a security issue, contact us at security@sixcyber.com.
