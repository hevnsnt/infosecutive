Security & Privacy Policy

At SixCyber, we are committed to safeguarding your privacy and maintaining the security of our digital infrastructure. This policy outlines our approach to security vulnerability disclosures and how we handle your personal information.

Security Disclosure Policy

We welcome reports of potential vulnerabilities affecting our website or services. If you identify a security issue, please notify us through the contact information provided in our security.txt file.

Responsible Disclosure

We request that researchers avoid actions that could disrupt our services or expose user data during testing. Please allow us reasonable time to investigate and resolve any issues before publicly disclosing them.

Confidentiality

Information shared with us during a vulnerability report will be treated as confidential. Your identity and contact details will never be disclosed, sold, or shared. They will only be used for communication regarding the report and internal remediation.

Privacy Policy

Data Collection

SixCyber only collects personal data that is voluntarily provided, such as contact information submitted via forms or emails. We do not use third-party trackers or collect unnecessary data.

Data Use

Any personal information collected is used exclusively to communicate with you and provide services requested. It will not be used for marketing, profiling, or unrelated business purposes.

Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with any third parties. All information remains strictly internal and is accessible only to authorized personnel.

Data Security

We take appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your data against unauthorized access, disclosure, or destruction.

Policy Updates

This policy may be updated periodically. All changes will be posted on this page, and we encourage you to review it regularly.

Contact

If you have questions about this policy or wish to report a security issue, please contact us at security@sixcyber.com.
