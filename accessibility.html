Website Accessibility Statement

SixCyber, Inc. is committed to ensuring our website is accessible to everyone, including people with disabilities. We strive to follow best practices and standards as defined by Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG) 2.1 Level AA to make our content perceivable, operable, understandable, and robust for all users.

Our efforts: We are continuously working to improve the accessibility of SixCyber.com. This includes using semantic HTML markup, providing descriptive alternative text for images, ensuring adequate color contrast, and enabling keyboard navigation throughout our site. We test our pages regularly using automated tools and manual checks to address any accessibility issues that arise. Our goal is to create a seamless and inclusive experience.

Continuous Improvement: While we have taken steps to enhance accessibility, we recognize that some areas of our website may still need improvement. For example, certain PDF resources or third-party video content we link to might not yet be fully accessible. We are in the process of reviewing these materials and will provide accessible alternatives (such as text transcripts or summaries) wherever possible.

Feedback and Assistance: If you experience any difficulty accessing any part of our website, or if you have suggestions on how we can improve accessibility, please do not hesitate to contact us. We welcome your feedback. You can reach our team via email at accessibility@sixcyber.com or by phone at 1-800-XXX-XXXX. We will do our best to assist you and resolve any issues.

SixCyber is dedicated to ongoing compliance with accessibility standards. As cybersecurity educators, we believe technology should empower everyone. We are committed to making our training and resources available to all business leaders, including those with disabilities. Thank you for visiting SixCyber.com.
