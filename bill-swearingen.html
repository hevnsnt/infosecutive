With over 20 years of frontline cybersecurity experience, Bill Swearingen has protected everything from small Kansas City businesses to Fortune 150 corporations and national telecom infrastructure. Philosophy & Approach

"Be nice"

"The harder you work, the luckier you get"

— Bill Swearingen

Bill's approach to cybersecurity is rooted in two fundamental principles that have guided his 20+ year career. The philosophy of "Be nice" reflects his belief that cybersecurity is ultimately about people — building trust, fostering collaboration, and creating security cultures where everyone feels empowered to do the right thing.

"The harder you work, the luckier you get" embodies his commitment to thorough preparation, continuous learning, and putting in the effort required to stay ahead of evolving threats. In cybersecurity, there are no shortcuts — only dedicated work and attention to detail.

Superpower

Bill's superpower is understanding how complex systems work and teaching those concepts clearly to others. Whether explaining advanced persistent threats to executives or helping technical teams implement zero-trust architectures, he bridges the gap between complex cybersecurity concepts and practical business needs.
