What is CMMC 2.0 compliance and do Kansas City defense contractors need it?

CMMC (Cybersecurity Maturity Model Certification) 2.0 is mandatory for all Department of Defense contractors handling Controlled Unclassified Information (CUI). Kansas City area defense contractors must achieve CMMC compliance by 2026. SixCyber's CMMC Registered Practitioners help organizations prepare for and maintain compliance.
