SixCyber provides comprehensive cybersecurity services including advanced testing, crisis response, executive leadership, compliance excellence, security operations, and strategic advisory. Investment in professional security testing typically ranges from $3,000 to $15,000 depending on scope and complexity. This includes comprehensive network and application assessments, social engineering evaluations, and detailed remediation guidance. Studies consistently show that proactive testing reduces incident costs by up to 80%, making it one of the most cost-effective security investments available.

SixCyber was founded by Bill Swearingen, former CenturyLink CISO and co-founder of SecKC. Our team has guided organizations through world-class cyber events, managed enterprise security budgets exceeding $50M, and contributed to national cybersecurity strategy. We bring Fortune 150-level expertise to organizations of all sizes, with the understanding that exceptional cybersecurity requires exceptional experience.

CMMC (Cybersecurity Maturity Model Certification) 2.0 is mandatory for all Department of Defense contractors handling Controlled Unclassified Information (CUI). Compliance is required by 2026 for continued DoD contracting. Our CMMC Registered Practitioners, with security clearance experience and deep understanding of DoD requirements, help organizations prepare for and maintain compliance efficiently.

SixCyber provides 24/7 incident response with initial response within 1 hour for critical incidents. According to the Ponemon Institute, organizations that contain breaches in under 200 days save an average of $1.76 million compared to longer response times.

While headquartered in the metro area, SixCyber serves clients nationwide, especially for specialized services like compliance consulting and incident response. We provide remote services and travel for on-site assessments throughout the Midwest and beyond.
