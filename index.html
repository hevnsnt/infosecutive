A virtual CISO (vCISO) is a seasoned security executive who serves as your organization's Chief Information Security Officer on a fractional basis. It's ideal for mid-market companies that need strategic security leadership but aren't ready to hire a full-time CISO — a role that commands $250,000–$400,000+ annually. SixCyber's vCISO service is led by a former Fortune 500 CISO who managed a $50M+ security budget and a 217-person global security team.

The Cybersecurity Maturity Model Certification (CMMC) is a Department of Defense framework required for defense contractors handling Controlled Unclassified Information (CUI). If your organization bids on DoD contracts, you will need CMMC certification. SixCyber helps organizations prepare through gap analysis, remediation guidance, policy development, and ongoing compliance support.
