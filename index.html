What to Expect

This interactive workshop will demystify cybersecurity and give you executive-level control over your company’s cyber safety. We will begin by explaining the current threat landscape in plain English – you’ll learn who the bad actors are and what they’re after. Then we’ll show you how criminals operate, tools they use, and then how to spot and stop such attacks.

We will guide you through the essential protections every small business should have (such as firewalls, antivirus, backups, strong passwords, multi-factor authentication, etc.), following the official guidance from the Small Business Administration and cybersecurity authorities. You’ll also witness a step-by-step walkthrough of a recent incident that we responded to and learn how a swift, prepared response can drastically limit the damage. Imagine knowing exactly what to do if your company laptop is stolen or if you discover malware on your system – this training gives you that clarity.

By the end of the session, you will have a personalized to-do list of steps to immediately tighten your defences, from instituting routine software updates and employee phishing drills, to drafting a simple incident response procedure. We’ll also supply you with policy templates (for example, a ready-made IT use policy and incident response plan) that you can adopt in your company. Throughout the training, you’re encouraged to ask questions and share concerns; we are all in it together and can learn from each other as well.

You’ll leave this training with newfound confidence as a business leader in charge of cybersecurity. No longer will cyber threats feel like an overwhelming IT problem. You will understand the key points to discuss with your IT team or service providers, and you’ll be equipped to make informed decisions to protect your business’s future. Every attendee receives a Certificate of Completion, along with a packet of resources including step-by-step security checklists and contacts for free follow-up support (like your local SBDC or cyber consultants). More importantly, you’ll take away peace of mind, knowing that you have proactively taken steps to guard your company’s assets and reputation.

F.A.Q

Frequently Asked Questions

  • Do I need a technical background to attend this bootcamp?

    No. This course is specifically designed for non-technical business leaders. We explain concepts in plain language and focus on what you need to know to lead cybersecurity efforts—not how to configure firewalls.

  • What will I be able to do after completing the training?

    You’ll walk away with a clear action plan for protecting your business, including templates, checklists, and incident response guidance. You’ll also know how to evaluate your IT provider, lead employee training, and reduce risk across your operations.

  • Is this training relevant if I already have an IT company or managed service provider (MSP)?

    Yes! Many breaches happen even when companies have IT support. This bootcamp teaches you how to ask the right questions, hold your vendors accountable, and ensure that critical protections (like backups, MFA, and monitoring) are truly in place.

  • Will I receive materials or resources I can use in my business?

    Absolutely. Every attendee receives editable cybersecurity policies, a step-by-step response plan, the “Mandatory 10” checklist, printable awareness posters, and a Certificate of Completion. You also get access to our exclusive online support group.

  • How much does it cost, and what’s included?

    The full-day bootcamp costs $899 and includes expert instruction, live hacking demonstrations, all printed materials, lunch, networking opportunities, and post-event resources. Group discounts and nonprofit rates may be available—just ask.

  • What if I have more questions or want a security assessment afterward?

    We’re here to help. Graduates gain access to optional follow-up services like penetration testing, staff training, or one-on-one strategy sessions. We can also connect you with vetted local security professionals or government support programs.
