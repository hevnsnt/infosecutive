About SixCyber

About SixCyber

Bill Swearingen founded SixCyber after seeing too many companies devastated by breaches that basic controls could have prevented. With more than twenty years of frontline experience, he has built and led teams in incident response, threat intelligence, and security product development. He has crafted national cybersecurity strategies for Governments in preparation to defend against cyber war.

SixCyber harnesses this unmatched expertise and network to provide enterprise grade protection through a simple per user subscription. As co-founder of SecKC, the world’s largest monthly information security meetup, we tap Kansas City’s top talent, keep all work onshore, and close the gap between uptime focused IT and true security. Rest easy knowing you have expert defense backed by proven leadership every hour of every day.
