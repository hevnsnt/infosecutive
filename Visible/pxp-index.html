About SixCyber

Mission: Enterprise-grade cybersecurity for Kansas City SMBs via local experts.

Founder & CEO: Bill Swearingen

Bill Swearingen is an industry leader in cyber defense strategy, with over 18 years of experience in both technical and executive cybersecurity roles. He has devoted his career to building and protecting critical infrastructure and Fortune 100 companies from advanced threats. Bill brings direct experience from ‘hands on keyboard’ work on the front lines and an in-depth understanding of the challenges security teams face. He has experience combating and dismantling the largest and most advanced threats, often at an internet-wide scale.

Prior to founding SixCyber, Bill led IronNet’s Global Incident Response and Red Teaming services, delivering outstanding cyber-related services and leading crisis response activities. Bill is passionate about cybersecurity and is recognized for founding SecKC and serving as former President of the Kansas City Chapter FBI Infragard. He has spoken at DEF CON and has crafted national cybersecurity strategies for governments.

Key Accomplishments: Founder of SecKC, Former President of the Kansas City Chapter FBI Infragard, DEF CON Speaker
