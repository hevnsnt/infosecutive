About the Bootcamp

Cybersecurity is crucial for all businesses, regardless of size. Customer and client information, payment data, and personal files are irreplaceable and dangerous in the hands of cybercriminals. This is the only cybersecurity course designed by industry experts specifically for SMB executives.

Our experts have crafted this executive bootcamp to arm you with everything needed to protect your business. Criminals use automated tools to scan and attack thousands of small businesses. Most SMBs have fewer resources to invest in security—this makes you the prime target. But not after this course.
